Chicken Noodle Soup

Delicious soup for a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Noodles
  • 10 Cups of Chicken Broth
  • 3 Cups of Diced Chicken
  • 1 Cup of Chopped Onions
  • 1 Cup of Chopped Celery
  • 1 Cup of Chopped Carrots

Instructions

  1. Using a large pot of water, boil the pasta. Drain the water when pasta is finished cooking
  2. In a seperate pot, boil the chicken broth and add in the vegetables. Reduce heat, cover and simmer.
  3. Stir in noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
