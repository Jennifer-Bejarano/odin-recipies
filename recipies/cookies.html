Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Warm and chewy chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 1/2 TSP of Baking Soda
  • 1/2 TSP of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
    • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 TBSP of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups of Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Mix the sugar, brown sugar, butter, egg, vanilla extract, flour, and chocolate chips.
  2. Scoop dough and place on a greased baking sheet
  3. Bake at 325 degrees for 15 to 17 minutes.
