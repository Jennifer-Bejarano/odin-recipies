Honey Salmon

Description

A simple and hearty baked dish with a touch of ginger.

The dish combines the sweetness of honey and saltiness of soy sauce to create a pleasant, yet savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Honey
  • 3 TBSP of Soy Sauce
  • 4 Salmon Filets
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Green Onions
  • 3 Cloves of Chopped Garlic
  • 2 TBSP of Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 TBSP of Powdered Ginger

Instructions

  1. Combine and stir the honey, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, and half of the green onions.
  2. Brush 3/4 of the mix on the salmon filets.
  3. Bake the filets in the oven at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove filets from oven and brush the remaining mix on the filets.
  5. Bake the filets again at 375 degrees for 5 minutes.
